# Mage: The Ascension — Player Primer

> A quick reference for this system: concept, and the lists you'll reach for most.

## Concept

Mage: The Ascension demos should be about belief colliding with reality. The app should support paradigms, practices, instruments, spheres, rotes, quintessence, paradox, chantries, mentors, and ideological enemies without flattening magic into a spell list.
