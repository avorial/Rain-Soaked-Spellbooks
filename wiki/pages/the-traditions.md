# The Traditions

> A fractious alliance of mystics defending wonder from grey conformity.

## Goals

- Secure influence over [[The Loft Chantry]] before rivals realize what is at stake.
- Control access to the [[The Talisman Drive]] without being blamed for the fallout.
- Use [[Cypher]] as an asset, scapegoat, or bargaining chip.
- Keep the truth of [[The Technocracy]] contained until containment stops being possible.

## Notable Members

- [[Cypher]] — Void Engineer defector

## Reach

- Operates out of [[The Loft Chantry]].

## Complications

- Entangled with [[The Technocracy]].

## Methods

- Offer useful favors with hooks in them.
- Control information before controlling territory.
- Put a friendly face between the party and the ugly order.
- Escalate quietly first, then publicly once leverage is secure.
