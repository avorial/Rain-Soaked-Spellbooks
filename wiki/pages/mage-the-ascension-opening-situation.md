# Mage: The Ascension Opening Situation

> A first playable scene tying the sample pages together.

## Start Here

The party arrives at [[The Loft Chantry]] because [[Cypher]] sent a warning, a job offer, a confession, or a plea for help. [[The Traditions]] is already moving, the [[The Talisman Drive]] has become dangerous, and [[The Technocracy]] is still deniable for one more scene.

## Immediate Questions

- Why is [[Jax]] the person who cannot ignore this?
- What does [[Cypher]] refuse to say in public?
- What does [[The Traditions]] offer that is genuinely useful?
- What sign of [[The Technocracy]] proves this is more than local trouble?

## First Three Scenes

1. **Public pressure:** something obvious happens at [[The Loft Chantry]] while witnesses are present.
2. **Private ask:** [[Cypher]] explains just enough to make refusal costly.
3. **Complication:** [[The Traditions]] arrives, claims authority, or reveals they are already one step ahead.

## Outcomes To Track

- Who leaves with the [[The Talisman Drive]]?
- Who learns the first real truth about [[The Technocracy]]?
- Which relationship is strained before the next session?
- What page should be created next: a map, a quest, a rival, or a secret history?
